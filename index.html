Our Mission Statement

The mission of the Chapin High School Navy JROTC Booster Club is to
promote and support the Navy JROTC program and Chapin High School.
We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization made up of parents and other
interested adults from the Chapin community. We raise funds to enhance
and expand the academic and extracurricular activities of the program.
